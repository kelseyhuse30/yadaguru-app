Welcome to YadaGuru! We are a free app built by Civic Hackers.

YadaGuru helps you remember everything you have to do for your college applucations and bugs you with texts to get it done!

To get started, enter the school's name.

Next, get the application submission date from the school you're applying to.

TIP: You'll find this on the school's undergraduate admissions page.

Enter the school's submission date, and click 'Next'.

One more question. Are you applying to an arts program?
